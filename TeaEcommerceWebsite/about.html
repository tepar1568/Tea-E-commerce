About Kooky for Tea

Our Mission

Kooky for Tea was founded in 1999 by Gypsy Anderson. She had the idea to create a e-commerce website for people who are eccentric and have a flair for interesting accessories as well as tea.

Kooky for Tea looks all around the world in search for exotic and eccentric items associated with tea for their customers. We also deliver our goodies all around the world, so know one is exempt. Everyone deserves to have a taste of our exquisite tea and our eloquent tea accessories.
