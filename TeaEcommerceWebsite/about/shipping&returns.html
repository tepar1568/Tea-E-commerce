Shipping

United States and Canada Shipping

Orders over $40 to the United States and Canada are free via USPS. Small orders ship for a flate rate of $5. United States order take one to four business days to arrive once your complete order is in stock.

International Shipping

Orders over $90 to the rest of the world are free via USPS. Small orders ship for a flate rate of $20. United States order take six to twelve business days to arrive once your complete order is in stock.

Returns

If your item(s) was deliverd to you damaged, you can contact us at our contact page and we will re-send you what you ordered. If you didn't like what you ordered or it wasn't what you expected, after you return the items you will have store credit.
